About us

Are you looking for a sustainably sourced and independently owned wine brand?

Look no further than Prosecco Ventures, a London-based company that proudly produces and markets a portfolio of high-quality Italian wines. As a family-owned business, we take great pride in every bottle that bears our name, and we're committed to bringing you the best of what Italy has to offer.

Fall in love with the charm of Bella Principessa, Signorina,and Bella Vino - three enchanting brands sure to win you over.

04

Our Lion logo

Prosecco Ventures features a variation of the Veneto lion as its company logo in tribute to the region's rich history and cultural identity, which dates back centuries.

The winged lion symbolizes strength, resilience, and a dedication to excellence and innovation. Using this emblem, we honor the traditions of Veneto and celebrate its enduring legacy.

CERTIFIED QUALITY

Counterfeit Prosecco is on the rise. Verify authenticity by looking for the D.O.C.G. or D.O.C. certification sticker on the back of every bottle that guarantees traditional production and excellent taste. D.O.C.G. signifies the highest quality.
