GUILT-FREE LUXURY PROSECCO

Bella Principessa is an impeccably well-presented, naturally refreshing, delicious, low-calorie, guilt-free luxury Prosecco and Prosecco Rosé that won't weigh you down. It's light and vibrant with lasting, persistent, gentle bubbles. Suitable for vegans, vegetarians, and celiacs.

Celebrate good taste in every sip and feel the pride of serving Bella Principessa to family and friends.

DRESS TO IMPRESS

Elevate your style with Bella Principessa, a well-priced, one-of-a-kind fairytale-inspired brand that stands out in a sea of dull, mass-produced prosecco options. It'schic, fun, and unforgettable - just like you!

Our single-estate Prosecco and Prosecco Rosé are 100% natural, vegan, and gluten-free, low in calories and sulfites, making them a healthier choice.
